Bathstone Restorations

Specialist Bathstone Restoration Services

Bathstone is a beautiful and iconic material, known for its warm appearance and historical significance. At Heritage Stone Cardiff, we specialise in restoring Bathstone masonry to its former glory, using gentle, traditional methods that respect the age and integrity of the stone. Whether your property has suffered from weathering, pollution, or structural damage, we can carefully clean, repair, and repoint to achieve lasting, sympathetic restoration.

Our team works on both listed and modern properties, ensuring every project meets the highest standards of craftsmanship. Contact us now for a free assessment and let us help preserve your Bathstone building for generations to come.

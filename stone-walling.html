Stone Walling

Crafted Stone Walls With Heritage Detail

At Heritage Stone Cardiff, we specialise in building and restoring stone walls that last generations. Whether you're after a traditional dry stone wall, a mortared boundary wall, or a structural retaining wall, we bring precision, experience, and craftsmanship to every build.

We use locally sourced stone when possible, ensuring that every project complements its environment and maintains the character of South Wales’ architectural style. All walls are constructed with durability and heritage in mind.

Types of Stone Walling We Offer

Dry Stone Walling

  • Mortar-free, traditional craftsmanship
  • Ideal for garden features or farmland
  • Excellent drainage and environmental benefit
  • Built to historic standards
  • Natural appearance with local stone

Mortared Walls

  • Durable boundary and retaining walls
  • Precision-built using lime mortar or modern blends
  • Great for driveways, gardens, or listed buildings
  • Blends with historic or modern properties
  • Can incorporate decorative stonework

Wall Repairs

  • Repair collapsed or damaged stone walls
  • Rebuild fallen sections with original or matching stone
  • Address leaning or structurally unsafe walls
  • Replace broken copings and joints
  • Heritage-compliant methods

Build Something That Lasts

From dry stone borders to full retaining walls, Heritage Stone Cardiff delivers strong, stunning results tailored to your landscape.

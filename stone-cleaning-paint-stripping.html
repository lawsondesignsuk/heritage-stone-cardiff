Stone Cleaning & Paint Stripping

Gentle & Effective Stone Cleaning Services

Over time, pollution, moss, and outdated paintwork can hide the true beauty of your stone building. At Heritage Stone Cardiff, we use traditional and modern cleaning techniques to safely remove dirt, staining, and paint without damaging the original masonry.

We assess every project individually to ensure the right method is used—whether it's DOFF steam cleaning, low-pressure washing, or specialist paint removal. Our process respects the heritage of your property and enhances its long-term durability and aesthetic appeal.

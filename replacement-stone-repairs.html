Replacement Stone & Repairs

Repair and Restore Damaged or Missing Stone

At Heritage Stone Cardiff, we’re experts at restoring the original beauty and structure of damaged stonework. Whether a lintel has cracked, a coping has crumbled, or stone elements are missing completely, we can hand-carve and install accurate replacements that blend seamlessly with your building’s character.

From heritage properties to modern builds, our team uses traditional and modern techniques to ensure repairs are durable, sympathetic, and visually perfect.

Request a Repair Quote

Our Stone Repair Services Include:

Cracked & Broken Stone

  • Repair fractures with lime-based methods
  • Remove and replace shattered elements
  • Stitching and consolidation techniques
  • Weatherproof finishes
  • Subtle colour blending

Stone Replacement

  • Custom-cut replacement stones
  • Match shape, texture, and colour
  • Bathstone, Pennant, sandstone & more
  • Historic restoration and repair
  • Installed using heritage methods

Decorative Feature Repairs

  • Rebuild damaged cills, lintels, or cornices
  • Restore architectural features like arches
  • Hand-finish detail work
  • Sourced stone to match surrounding masonry
  • Ideal for listed or period properties

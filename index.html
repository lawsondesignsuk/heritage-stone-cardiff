Expert Stone Masonry Services in Cardiff – Bath Stone Restoration, Repairs & More

Stone & Brickwork Repointing

Our stone and brickwork repointing services restore the integrity of your masonry by carefully removing old, deteriorated mortar. We replace it with high-quality, durable mortar that ensures lasting strength and an improved visual appeal for your property.

Bath Stone Restorations

Bring the charm of bath stone back to life with our expert restoration services. We specialise in the repair, cleaning, and preservation of bath stone, ensuring its natural beauty is maintained for years to come.

Stone Cleaning & Paint Stripping

Restore your stonework with our professional cleaning and paint stripping services. We use safe and effective techniques to remove dirt, stains, and old paint, revealing the natural beauty of your masonry for a fresh, timeless look.

Stone Walling

Whether it's for garden walls or structural features, our stone walling services create stunning, durable walls that enhance the character and beauty of your property. Our custom-built walls are designed to stand the test of time while complementing your surroundings.

Replacement Stone & Repairs

Our stone replacement and repair services restore damaged or weathered masonry to its former glory. We carefully select matching stones and employ expert techniques to ensure a flawless, seamless finish, preserving the authenticity and structural integrity of your property.
